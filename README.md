# Hand-held-working-fan
A mini hand held fan with electronics
runs using a 9v battery, (toy) mini dc motor

<img width="650" height="615" alt="Screenshot 2026-07-13 162140" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/0de0d5bf-6d68-484e-903d-637763a2ef00" />

Instructions:

print using pla and add supports

glue the fan holder to the bottom fan cover

Glue the fan ring to the bottom fan cover

place the motor inside the bottom cover slot and glue it in place

connect the battery to the battery wire

Place the battery inside the handle slot for the battery

guide the black battery wire directly to one side of the motors metal thin sheet and connect it

place the switch on the handle slot for the switch and connect the red wire to it

connect the other wire of the switch to the other side of the motor

(if the fan is blowing in the opposite direction, change the side of the motor and connect the wires again)

place the top cover ontop of the bottom cover(zip ties can be used to secure it into place however it is optional)

close the battery lid, and glue the handle grip(the zig zag one) to the handle.
